15.1 – 15.2

Qo'sh Integrallar — Double Integrals

Nazariya

Qo'sh integral ta'rifi (§15.1)

Bitta o'zgaruvchili integralda biz \( [a, b] \) kesmani \( n \) ta kichik kesmaga bo'lib Riman summasi hosil qilamiz. Qo'sh integralda esa to'rtburchak \( R = [a,b] \times [c,d] \) ni kichik to'rtburchaklarga bo'lamiz. Har bir kichik to'rtburchak \( R_{ij} \) da namunaviy nuqta \( (x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \) tanlaymiz va Riman summasini hosil qilamiz:

Qo'sh Riman summasi
\[ \sum_{i=1}^m \sum_{j=1}^n f(x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \Delta A \]
Qo'sh integral ta'rifi (limiti)
\[ \iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \lim_{m,n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^m \sum_{j=1}^n f(x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \Delta A \]
Geometrik Ma'no

Agar \( f(x,y) \ge 0 \) bo'lsa, qo'sh integral \( z = f(x,y) \) sirt ostidagi hajmni ifodalaydi.

Teorema

Fubini Teoremasi (§15.1)

Agar \( f \) funksiya \( R = [a,b] \times [c,d] \) sohada uzluksiz bo'lsa, u holda qo'sh integralni takrorlangan (iterative) integral sifatida hisoblash mumkin:

Fubini Teoremasi
\[ \iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \int_a^b \left[ \int_c^d f(x,y) \, dy \right] dx = \int_c^d \left[ \int_a^b f(x,y) \, dx \right] dy \]

Tartib muhim emas — natija bir xil chiqadi.

Kitob Misoli 15.1 — Paraboloid hajmi

\( z = 16 - x^2 - 2y^2, \quad R = [0,2] \times [0,2] \) — hajmni taxminlang

\( m = n = 2 \) bo'linish bilan yuqori o'ng burchak nuqtalarini olamiz:

  1. \( \Delta A = 1 \) (har bir to'rtburchak yuzasi)
  2. \( f(1,1) = 16 - 1 - 2 = 13 \) (R₁₁ to'rtburchagi)
  3. \( f(1,2) = 16 - 1 - 8 = 7 \) (R₁₂)
  4. \( f(2,1) = 16 - 4 - 2 = 10 \) (R₂₁)
  5. \( f(2,2) = 16 - 4 - 8 = 4 \) (R₂₂)
  6. \( V \approx 13(1) + 7(1) + 10(1) + 4(1) = 34 \) (aniq javob: \( V = 48 \))
Kitob Misoli — Fubini bilan hisoblash

\( \iint_R (x - 3y^2) \, dA \), bunda \( R = \{(x,y) \mid 0 \le x \le 2, \ 1 \le y \le 2\} \)

  1. Avval y bo'yicha integrallaymiz (x = const):
    \[ \int_1^2 (x - 3y^2) \, dy = \left[ xy - y^3 \right]_1^2 = (2x - 8) - (x - 1) = x - 7 \]
  2. So'ng x bo'yicha integrallaymiz:
    \[ \int_0^2 (x - 7) \, dx = \left[ \frac{x^2}{2} - 7x \right]_0^2 = (2 - 14) - 0 = -12 \]
Javob: \(-12\)
15.2 — Nazariya

Umumiy Soha D ustidagi qo'sh integral

Ixtiyoriy D soha uchun 2 xil tur mavjud:

I-tur soha (x bo'yicha cheklangan)
\[ D = \{ (x,y) \mid a \le x \le b, \ g_1(x) \le y \le g_2(x) \} \] \[ \iint_D f(x,y) \, dA = \int_a^b \int_{g_1(x)}^{g_2(x)} f(x,y) \, dy \, dx \]
II-tur soha (y bo'yicha cheklangan)
\[ D = \{ (x,y) \mid c \le y \le d, \ h_1(y) \le x \le h_2(y) \} \] \[ \iint_D f(x,y) \, dA = \int_c^d \int_{h_1(y)}^{h_2(y)} f(x,y) \, dx \, dy \]
Kitob Misoli 15.2 — Umumiy sohada

\( z = x^2 + y^2, \quad D = \{ y = 2x \text{ va } y = x^2 \text{ orasida} \} \) hajmni toping

  1. Kesishish nuqtasini topamiz: \( 2x = x^2 \implies x = 0 \) va \( x = 2 \). Demak \( 0 \le x \le 2 \), \( x^2 \le y \le 2x \).
  2. Integral yozamiz (I-tur):
    \[ V = \int_0^2 \int_{x^2}^{2x} (x^2 + y^2) \, dy \, dx \]
  3. y bo'yicha integrallaymiz:
    \[ \int_{x^2}^{2x} (x^2 + y^2) \, dy = \left[ x^2y + \frac{y^3}{3} \right]_{x^2}^{2x} = \left(2x^3 + \frac{8x^3}{3}\right) - \left(x^4 + \frac{x^6}{3}\right) \]
  4. x bo'yicha integrallaymiz:
    \[ \int_0^2 \left( \frac{14x^3}{3} - x^4 - \frac{x^6}{3} \right) dx = \left[ \frac{14x^4}{12} - \frac{x^5}{5} - \frac{x^7}{21} \right]_0^2 = \frac{216}{35} \]
Javob: V = \(\frac{216}{35}\)
15.3

Polar Koordinatlar

Nazariya

Polar koordinataga o'tish

Agar D doira bo'lsa, \( x = r \cos\theta, y = r \sin\theta \) orqali o'tamiz. Muhim: \( dA = r \, dr \, d\theta \).

Polar koordinatada qo'sh integral
\[ \iint_D f(x,y) \, dA = \int_\alpha^\beta \int_{h_1(\theta)}^{h_2(\theta)} f(r \cos\theta, r \sin\theta) \, r \, dr \, d\theta \]
⚠️

Eng ko'p kechadigan xato: \( dA = dr \, d\theta \) deb yozish. To'g'ri shakli: \( dA = \mathbf{r} \, dr \, d\theta \).

Polar misol — Doira ustidagi integral

\( \iint_D (3x + 4y^2) \, dA \), bu yerda \( D = \{x^2 + y^2 \le 4, \ y \ge 0\} \)

  1. Chegaralar polar da: \( 0 \le \theta \le \pi, \quad 0 \le r \le 2 \)
  2. x va y ni almashtirish: \( 3x + 4y^2 = 3r \cos\theta + 4r^2 \sin^2\theta \)
  3. Integral yozamiz:
    \[ \int_0^\pi \int_0^2 (3r \cos\theta + 4r^2 \sin^2\theta) \, r \, dr \, d\theta = \int_0^\pi \int_0^2 (3r^2 \cos\theta + 4r^3 \sin^2\theta) \, dr \, d\theta \]
  4. r bo'yicha hisoblash:
    \[ \int_0^\pi \left[ r^3 \cos\theta + r^4 \sin^2\theta \right]_0^2 d\theta = \int_0^\pi (8 \cos\theta + 16 \sin^2\theta) \, d\theta \]
  5. θ bo'yicha hisoblash (\( \sin^2\theta = \frac{1-\cos2\theta}{2} \)):
    \[ \left[ 8 \sin\theta + 8\theta - 4 \sin 2\theta \right]_0^\pi = 0 + 8\pi - 0 = 8\pi \]
Javob: \( 8\pi \)
15.6

Uch O'lchovli Integrallar — Triple Integrals

Nazariya

Triple integral ta'rifi (§15.6)

Fazoviy qism (parallelopiped) uchun:

Triple integral limit ta'rifi
\[ \iiint_B f(x,y,z) \, dV = \lim_{l,m,n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^l \sum_{j=1}^m \sum_{k=1}^n f(x_{ijk}^*, y_{ijk}^*, z_{ijk}^*) \Delta V \]
Fubini Teoremasi (triple)
\[ \iiint_B f(x,y,z) \, dV = \int_r^s \int_c^d \int_a^b f(x,y,z) \, dx \, dy \, dz \]
Kitob Misoli 15.6.1 — To'g'ri parallelepiped

\( \iiint_B xyz^2 \, dV, \quad B = \{0 \le x \le 1, \ -1 \le y \le 2, \ 0 \le z \le 3\} \)

  1. x bo'yicha: \( \int_0^1 xyz^2 \, dx = \left[ \frac{x^2yz^2}{2} \right]_0^1 = \frac{yz^2}{2} \)
  2. y bo'yicha: \( \int_{-1}^2 \frac{yz^2}{2} \, dy = \left[ \frac{y^2z^2}{4} \right]_{-1}^2 = z^2 - \frac{z^2}{4} = \frac{3z^2}{4} \)
  3. z bo'yicha: \( \int_0^3 \frac{3z^2}{4} \, dz = \left[ \frac{z^3}{4} \right]_0^3 = \frac{27}{4} \)
Javob: \(\frac{27}{4}\)
Kitob Misoli — Umumiy soha ustida

\( \iiint_E z \, dV, \quad E = \{0 \le x \le 1, \ 0 \le y \le \sqrt{1-x^2}, \ 0 \le z \le \sqrt{1-x^2}\} \)

  1. z bo'yicha: \( \int_0^{\sqrt{1-x^2}} z \, dz = \left[\frac{z^2}{2}\right]_0^{\sqrt{1-x^2}} = \frac{1-x^2}{2} \)
  2. y bo'yicha: \( \int_0^{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \frac{1-x^2}{2} \, dy = \frac{1-x^2}{2} \sqrt{1-x^2} = \frac{(1-x^2)^{3/2}}{2} \)
  3. x bo'yicha (\(x = \sin\theta\)):
    \[ \int_0^1 \frac{(1-x^2)^{3/2}}{2} dx = \frac{1}{2} \int_0^{\pi/2} \cos^4\theta \, d\theta = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{3\pi}{16} = \frac{3\pi}{32} \]
Javob: \(\frac{3\pi}{32}\)
15.7

Silindrli Koordinatlar

Nazariya

Silindrli koordinata o'zgarishi

Silindrli tizim
\[ x = r \cos\theta, \quad y = r \sin\theta, \quad z = z \] \[ dV = r \, dr \, d\theta \, dz \] \[ x^2 + y^2 = r^2 \]
Kitob Misoli 15.7.4 — Massa hisoblash

\( E: x^2+y^2 \le 1, \ 1-r^2 \le z \le 4 \), Zichlik \( \rho(x,y,z) = K\sqrt{x^2+y^2} = Kr \)

  1. Silindrli koordinatada chegaralar: \( 0 \le \theta \le \pi, \ 0 \le r \le 1, \ 1-r^2 \le z \le 4 \)
  2. Massa integrali:
    \[ m = \iiint_E K\sqrt{x^2+y^2} \, dV = \int_0^\pi \int_0^1 \int_{1-r^2}^4 Kr \cdot r \, dz \, dr \, d\theta \]
  3. z bo'yicha: \( \int_{1-r^2}^4 dz = 4 - (1-r^2) = 3 + r^2 \)
  4. r va θ bo'yicha:
    \[ K \int_0^\pi d\theta \cdot \int_0^1 r^2(3+r^2) \, dr = K\pi \left[ r^3 + \frac{r^5}{5} \right]_0^1 = K\pi \left(1 + \frac{1}{5}\right) = \frac{6K\pi}{5} \]
Javob: \( m = \frac{6K\pi}{5} \)
15.8

Sferik Koordinatlar

Nazariya

Sferik koordinata o'zgarishi

Sferik koordinatalar va Yakobian
\[ x = \rho \sin\phi \cos\theta, \quad y = \rho \sin\phi \sin\theta, \quad z = \rho \cos\phi \] \[ dV = \rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta \] \[ \rho^2 = x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \]
Kitob Misoli — Sfera hajmi

\( B: x^2+y^2+z^2 \le a^2 \) sferaning hajmini toping

  1. Sferik chegaralar: \( 0 \le \rho \le a, \ 0 \le \phi \le \pi, \ 0 \le \theta \le 2\pi \)
  2. Integral yozamiz:
    \[ V = \int_0^{2\pi} \int_0^\pi \int_0^a \rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta \]
  3. Hisoblash (alohida ko'paytmalar qilib ishlash mumkin): \[ \int_0^a \rho^2 d\rho = \frac{a^3}{3}, \quad \int_0^\pi \sin\phi d\phi = 2, \quad \int_0^{2\pi} d\theta = 2\pi \] Jami: \( \frac{a^3}{3} \cdot 2 \cdot 2\pi = \frac{4\pi a^3}{3} \)
Javob: \( V = \frac{4}{3}\pi a^3 \)
16.2

Skalyar Chiziq Integrali

Nazariya

Chiziq integralining ta'rifi (§16.2)

Chiziq integral ta'rifi va Parametrik ifodasi
\[ \int_C f(x,y) \, ds = \int_a^b f(x(t), y(t)) \sqrt{(x'(t))^2 + (y'(t))^2} \, dt \]
Fazoviy egri chiziq uchun

\[ \int_C f(x,y,z) \, ds = \int_a^b f(\mathbf{r}(t)) |\mathbf{r}'(t)| \, dt \]

Kitob Misoli 16.2.1 — Yarim doira

\( \int_C (2 + x^2y) \, ds, \quad C: x^2 + y^2 = 1 \) yuqori yarmi

  1. Parametrlash: \( x = \cos t, \ y = \sin t, \ 0 \le t \le \pi \)
  2. \( ds \) ni topamiz:
    \[ ds = \sqrt{(-\sin t)^2 + (\cos t)^2} \, dt = dt \]
  3. Hisoblash:
    \[ \int_0^\pi (2 + \cos^2 t \sin t) \, dt = \left[ 2t - \frac{\cos^3 t}{3} \right]_0^\pi = \left(2\pi + \frac{1}{3}\right) - \left(0 - \frac{1}{3}\right) = 2\pi + \frac{2}{3} \]
Javob: \( 2\pi + \frac{2}{3} \)
16.2 — Vektorli maydonlar

Vektor Chiziq Integrali — Ish (Work)

Nazariya

Vektor maydon bo'yicha chiziq integrali (§16.2)

Kuch maydoni bo'yicha chiziq integrali (ish)
\[ W = \int_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = \int_a^b \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r}(t)) \cdot \mathbf{r}'(t) \, dt \]
Kitob Misoli 16.2.7 — Bajarilgan Ish

\( \mathbf{F}(x,y) = x^2\mathbf{i} - xy\mathbf{j}, \quad C: \mathbf{r}(t) = \cos t\,\mathbf{i} + \sin t\,\mathbf{j}, \ 0 \le t \le \pi/2 \)

  1. \( \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r}(t)) \) ni topamiz: \( \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r}(t)) = \cos^2 t\,\mathbf{i} - \cos t \sin t\,\mathbf{j} \)
  2. \( \mathbf{r}'(t) \) topamiz: \( \mathbf{r}'(t) = -\sin t\,\mathbf{i} + \cos t\,\mathbf{j} \)
  3. Skalyar ko'paytma:
    \[ \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r}(t)) \cdot \mathbf{r}'(t) = (\cos^2 t)(-\sin t) + (-\cos t \sin t)(\cos t) = -2 \cos^2 t \sin t \]
  4. Integral hisoblash:
    \[ W = \int_0^{\pi/2} -2 \cos^2 t \sin t \, dt = \left[ \frac{2 \cos^3 t}{3} \right]_0^{\pi/2} = 0 - \frac{2}{3} = -\frac{2}{3} \]
Javob: W = \( -\frac{2}{3} \)
16.3

Chiziq Integrallar uchun Asos Teoremasi

Asosiy Teorema

Fundamental Theorem for Line Integrals (§16.3)

Agar \( \mathbf{F} = \nabla f \) konservativ maydon bo'lsa:

Chiziq Integrallar uchun Asos Teoremasi
\[ \int_C \nabla f \cdot d\mathbf{r} = f(\mathbf{r}(b)) - f(\mathbf{r}(a)) \]
Konservativ maydon belgilari

2D da: \( \mathbf{F} = \langle P,Q \rangle \) konservativ \( \iff \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} \)

3D da: \( \mathbf{F} \) konservativ \( \iff \text{curl } \mathbf{F} = \mathbf{0} \)

16.4

Green Teoremasi

Green Teoremasi (§16.4)

Green Teoremasi

Asosiy formula
\[ \oint_C P \, dx + Q \, dy = \iint_D \left( \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \right) dA \]
Green Teoremasining yordami bilan yuzani (Area) hisoblash:

\[ A(D) = \frac{1}{2} \oint_C x \, dy - y \, dx = \oint_C x \, dy = -\oint_C y \, dx \]

Kitob Misoli 16.4 — Green bilan soddalashtirish

\( \oint_C (x^4 + y) \, dx - (x + y^4) \, dy, \quad C: x^2 + 4y^2 = 4 \) ellipsi

  1. \( P = x^4+y, \quad Q = -x-y^4 \) deb belgilaymiz
  2. \( \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} = -1 - 1 = -2 \)
  3. Green Teoremasi qo'llaymiz:
    \[ \oint_C = \iint_D (-2) \, dA = -2 \cdot A(D) \]
  4. Ellips yuzasini topamiz: \( x^2/4 + y^2/1 \le 1 \), demak \( A = \pi \cdot 2 \cdot 1 = 2\pi \)
Javob: \( -4\pi \)
16.7

Yuza Integrallari — Surface Integrals

Nazariya — Yuza Integrali va Oqim (Flux)

Yuza integrallari formulalari (§16.7)

Skalyar yuza integrali (\( z = g(x,y) \))
\[ \iint_S f(x,y,z) \, dS = \iint_D f(x,y,g(x,y)) \sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right)^2} \, dA \]
Vektor yuza integrali (Flux) — yuqoriga yo'nalgan normal
\[ \iint_S \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iint_D \mathbf{F} \cdot \langle -g_x, -g_y, 1 \rangle \, dA \]
16.8 – 16.9

Stokes va Divergence Teoremalari

Stokes Teoremasi (§16.8)
Stokes Teoremasi
\[ \oint_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = \iint_S (\text{curl} \, \mathbf{F}) \cdot d\mathbf{S} \]

1D chiziq integralini o'rab turgan 2D fazoviy yuza integraliga bog'laydi.

Divergence Teoremasi (§16.9)
Divergence (Gauss) Teoremasi
\[ \iint_S \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iiint_E \text{div} \, \mathbf{F} \, dV \]

2D yopiq yuza integralini (Flux) ichidagi 3D hajmiy integralga bog'laydi.

Muhim Identikliklar (Qoidalar)
div va curl operatsiyalari
\[ \text{div}(\text{curl} \, \mathbf{F}) = \nabla \cdot (\nabla \times \mathbf{F}) = 0 \] \[ \text{curl}(\nabla f) = \nabla \times (\nabla f) = \mathbf{0} \] \[ \text{div} \mathbf{F} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z} \]
