15.1 – 15.2

Qo'sh Integrallar — Double Integrals

Nazariya

To'rtburchak soha ustida Riman summasi

Agar $R = [a, b] \times [c, d]$ bo'lsa, qo'sh integral kichik to'rtburchaklardagi limit qiymatidir:

Qo'sh integral limiti $$\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \lim_{m,n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^m \sum_{j=1}^n f(x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \, \Delta A$$

Fubini Teoremasi

Uzluksiz funksiyalar uchun integral tartibini almashtirish natijani o'zgartirmaydi:

$$\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \int_a^b \int_c^d f(x,y) \, dy \, dx = \int_c^d \int_a^b f(x,y) \, dx \, dy$$
15.3

Qutb Koordinatalari — Polar Coordinates

Nazariya

Soha doira yoki disk ko'rinishida bo'lganda, quyidagi o'zgaruvchilarni kiritish qulay:

$$x = r \cos\theta, \quad y = r \sin\theta, \quad x^2 + y^2 = r^2, \quad dA = r \, dr \, d\theta$$

Qutb koordinatasidagi to'liq integral ko'rinishi:

$$\iint_R f(x,y) \, dA = \int_{\alpha}^{\beta} \int_{a}^{b} f(r\cos\theta, r\sin\theta) \, r \, dr \, d\theta$$
15.6

Uch O'lchovli Integral — Triple Integrals

Kitob Misoli

Parallelepiped qutida integral: $B = [0, 1] \times [-1, 2] \times [0, 3]$

$$\iiint_B xh yz^2 \, dV = \int_0^1 x \, dx \int{-1}^2 y \, dy \int_0^3 z^2 \, dz$$

  1. $x$ bo'yicha integral: $\int_0^1 x \, dx = \left[\frac{x^2}{2}\right]_0^1 = \frac{1}{2}$
  2. $y$ bo'yicha: $\int_{-1}^2 y \, dy = \left[\frac{y^2}{2}\right]_{-1}^2 = \frac{4}{2} - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2}$
  3. $z$ bo'yicha: $\int_0^3 z^2 \, dz = \left[\frac{z^3}{3}\right]_0^3 = \frac{27}{3} = 9$
Javob: $\frac{1}{2} \times \frac{3}{2} \times 9 = \frac{27}{4}$
15.7

Silindrli Koordinatalar — Cylindrical Coordinates

Nazariya

Fazoviy jism simmetrik silindr, konus yoki paraboloid bo'lganda qo'llaniladi:

$$x = r \cos\theta, \quad y = r \sin\theta, \quad z = z, \quad dV = r \, dz \, dr \, d\theta$$
15.8

Sferik Koordinatalar — Spherical Coordinates

Nazariya

Sfera va markaziy simmetriyali sohalar uchun o'tish qoidalari:

$$x = \rho \sin\phi \cos\theta, \quad y = \rho \sin\phi \sin\theta, \quad z = \rho \cos\phi$$ $$\rho^2 = x^2 + y^2 + z^2, \quad dV = \rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta$$
16.2

Skalyar Chiziqli Integral — Scalar Line Integral

Nazariya

Egri chiziq yoyi $C$ bo'ylab skalyar funksiyani integrallash formulasi:

$$\int_C f(x,y,z) \, ds = \int_a^b f(x(t), y(t), z(t)) \sqrt{\left(\frac{dx}{dt}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{dy}{dt}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{dz}{dt}\right)^2} \, dt$$
16.2

Vektor Chiziqli Integral — Vector Line Integral

Nazariya

$\mathbf{F}$ vektor maydonining $C$ egri chiziq bo'ylab bajargan ishi:

$$\int_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = \int_a^b \mathbf{F}(\mathbf{r}(t)) \cdot \mathbf{r}'(t) \, dt = \int_C P \, dx + Q \, dy + R \, dz$$
16.3

Chiziqli Integral Asos Teoremasi — FTLI

Teorema

Agar $\mathbf{F} = \nabla f$ konservativ maydon bo'lsa, chiziqli integral yo'lga bog'liq bo'lmaydi:

$$\int_C \nabla f \cdot d\mathbf{r} = f(\mathbf{r}(b)) - f(\mathbf{r}(a))$$
16.4

Green Teoremasi — Green's Theorem

Teorema

Yopiq tekis kontur chizig'ini uning ichidagi ikki o'lchovli soha integrali bilan bog'laydi:

$$\oint_C P \, dx + Q \, dy = \iint_D \left( \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \right) dA$$

Green yordamida soha yuzasini hisoblash formulasi:

$$A = \frac{1}{2} \oint_C x \, dy - y \, dx$$
16.7

Sirt Integrali & Flux — Surface Integrals

Nazariya

Vektor maydonining $S$ sirt orqali o'tish oqimi (Flux):

$$\iint_S \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iint_D \mathbf{F} \cdot \langle -g_x, -g_y, 1 \rangle \, dA$$
16.8

Stokes Teoremasi — Stokes' Theorem

Teorema

Green teoremasining fazoviy 3D ko'rinishi:

$$\oint_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r} = \iint_S (\nabla \times \mathbf{F}) \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iint_S (\text{curl } \mathbf{F}) \cdot d\mathbf{S}$$

Rotor ($\text{curl } \mathbf{F}$) matritsa ko'rinishi:

$$\text{curl } \mathbf{F} = \nabla \times \mathbf{F} = \begin{vmatrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\ P & Q & R \end{vmatrix}$$
16.9

Divergensiya Teoremasi — Divergence Theorem

Teorema

Yopiq sirt integrali va fazoviy hajm integrali bog'liqligi (Gauss-Ostrogradsky):

$$\iint_S \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iiint_E (\nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}) \, dV = \iiint_E (\text{div } \mathbf{F}) \, dV$$

Divergensiya hisoblash formulasi:

$$\text{div } \mathbf{F} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z}$$

Koordinata Sistemalari va $dV / dA$ Elementlari

Sistema O'zgaruvchilar Element ($dA / dV$) Asosiy qo'llanilishi
Kartezian $x, y, z$ $dx \, dy \, dz$ To'g'ri to'rtburchaklar, prizmalar
Qutb (2D) $r, \theta$ $r \, dr \, d\theta$ Doiraviy va aylana sohalar
Silindrli $r, \theta, z$ $r \, dz \, dr \, d\theta$ Silindr va konus jismalari
Sferik $\rho, \phi, \theta$ $\rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta$ Sferalar va markaziy jismlar
Amaliyot

Stewart Calculus bo'yicha Interaktiv Test

1. To'rtburchak sohadagi qo'sh integral ta'rifi qaysi limit orqali ifodalanadi?
A $\lim \sum f(x_i^*) \Delta x$
B $\lim \sum \sum f(x_{ij}^*, y_{ij}^*) \Delta A$
C $\lim \sum \sum \sum f(x_{ijk}^*) \Delta V$
D $\lim \int f(x) dx$
To'g'ri! Ikki o'lchovli integral to'rtburchaklar yuzalari $\Delta A$ bo'yicha qo'shaloq limit hisoblanadi.
2. Qutb (polar) koordinata sistemasiga o'tishda yuza elementi $dA$ nimaga teng?
A $dr \, d\theta$
B $r \, dr \, d\theta$
C $r^2 \, dr \, d\theta$
D $\rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho$
To'g'ri! Yakobian koeffitsiyenti tufayli qutb koordinatasida har doim $dA = r \, dr \, d\theta$ bo'ladi.
3. Fubini teoremasining asosiy mazmuni nimalardan iborat?
A Integral har doim nolga teng bo'ladi
B Uzluksiz funksiyalarda integrallash tartibini o'zgartirish mumkin
C Qo'sh integral faqat musbat sohalarda mavjud
D Ichki integral doim faqat $y$ o'qi bo'yicha olinishi shart
To'g'ri! Fubini teoremasiga ko'ra uzluksiz funksiyalarda integrallash tartibini xohlagancha o'zgartirish mumkin.
4. $\mathbf{F} = \nabla f$ (konservativ maydon) bo'lsa, har qanday yopiq $C$ kontur uchun $\oint_C \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{r}$ qiymati nimaga teng?
A $1$
B $0$
C Yo'lning uzunligiga bog'liq
D $\infty$
To'g'ri! Konservativ maydonda yopiq trayektoriya bo'ylab chiziqli integral (bajarilgan ish) doimo $0$ ga teng.
5. Tekis plastinkaning massasini $\rho(x,y)$ zichlik funksiyasi yordamida qanday topish mumkin?
A $m = \iint_D \rho(x,y) \, dA$
B $m = \iint_D 1 \, dA$
C $m = \oint_C \rho(x,y) \, ds$
D $m = \text{div } \mathbf{F}$
To'g'ri! Massa zichlikning soha bo'yicha qo'sh integrali orqali aniqlanadi.
6. Green teoremasi qanday integrallarni o'zaro bog'laydi?
A Yopiq tekis chiziqli integralni tekis soha integrali bilan
B Sirt integralini hajm integrali bilan
C Ikki karrali integralni sferik integral bilan
D Skalyar chiziqli integralni vektor chiziqli integral bilan
To'g'ri! Green teoremasi tekislikdagi yopiq kontur integrali va shu kontur o'rab turgan tekis soha integralini bog'laydi.
7. Egri chiziqning yo'nalishi o'zgarganda skalyar chiziqli integral $\int_{-C} f(x,y) \, ds$ qiymati qanday o'zgaradi?
A Ishorasi qarama-qarshiga o'zgaradi
B Mutlaqo o'zgarmaydi ($\int_{-C} f \, ds = \int_C f \, ds$)
C Nolga teng bo'lib qoladi
D Ikki barobarga ortadi
To'g'ri! Skalyar integral yoy uzunligi $ds$ bo'yicha olingani uchun kontur yo'nalishiga bog'liq emas.
8. $\mathbf{F}(x,y) = \langle y, x \rangle$ vektor maydoni konservativmi?
A Ha, chunki $\frac{\partial P}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} = 1$
B Yo'q, chunki rotor noldan farqli
C Yo'q, chunki divergensiyasi nolga teng
D Buni aniqlashning imkoni yo'q
To'g'ri! Xususiy hosilalar o'zaro teng bo'lgani uchun ushbu maydon konservativ hisoblanadi.
9. Sferik koordinata sistemasida hajm elementi $dV$ nimaga teng?
A $\rho \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta$
B $\rho^2 \sin\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta$
C $r \, dz \, dr \, d\theta$
D $\rho^2 \cos\phi \, d\rho \, d\phi \, d\theta$
To'g'ri! Sferik integralda Yakobian koeffitsiyenti $\rho^2 \sin\phi$ ga teng bo'ladi.
10. Stokes teoremasi va Green teoremasi o'rtasidagi munosabat qanday?
A Green teoremasi Stokes teoremasining 2D tekislikdagi xususiy holidir
B Ular mutlaqo boshqa-boshqa tushunchalar
C Stokes teoremasi faqat skalyar maydonlar uchun o'rinli
D Green teoremasi faqat divergensiya bilan ishlaydi
To'g'ri! Agar Stokes teoremasini tekis $xy$ sirtida qo'llasak, u to'g'ridan-to'g'ri Green teoremasiga aylanadi.
11. Divergensiya teoremasi (Gauss) qachon va qanday sirtlarda qo'llaniladi?
A Har qanday ochiq paraboloid sirtlarda
B To'liq yopiq sirtlar (biron jism chegarasi) ustida
C Faqatgina radiusi 1 ga teng bo'lgan sferada
D Faqat $\text{div } \mathbf{F} = 0$ bo'lgan holatda
To'g'ri! Divergensiya teoremasi har doim ichki hajmga ega bo'lgan to'liq yopiq sirtlarda ishlaydi.
12. Agar $\mathbf{F} = \langle x, y, z \rangle$ bo'lsa, $\text{div } \mathbf{F}$ qiymati nechaga teng?
A $0$
B $1$
C $3$
D $x+y+z$
To'g'ri! $\frac{\partial x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial z} = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3$.
13. Sirt maydoni (Surface Area) formulasida $dS$ differensiali qaysi radikal ko'rinishga ega?
A $\sqrt{1 + g_x^2 + g_y^2} \, dA$
B $\sqrt{g_x^2 + g_y^2} \, dA$
C $(1 + g_x + g_y) \, dA$
D $\rho^2 \sin\phi$
To'g'ri! Sirt maydonining proyeksiyadagi differensiali shu radikal yordamida aniqlanadi.
14. Har qanday konservativ maydonning rotori ($\text{curl } \mathbf{F}$) nimaga teng?
A $\mathbf{0}$ (Nol vektor)
B Maydonning skalyar potensialiga
C $3$ ga
D Divergensiyaning teskari qiymatiga
To'g'ri! Konservativ maydon uyursiz (irrotational) bo'lgani uchun uning rotori doim nolga teng.
15. $\iiint_E 1 \, dV$ integrali geometrik jihatdan nimani anglatadi?
A $E$ jismning massasini
B Jismning og'irlik markazini
C $E$ fazoviy jismning hajmini
D Sirt yuzasini
To'g'ri! Integrand funktsiya 1 ga teng bo'lganda, uch o'lchovli integral soha hajmini beradi.
